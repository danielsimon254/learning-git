Welcome to the Solar System!

Mercury

The smallest planet in our solar system and closest to the Sun.

Venus

Similar in structure to Earth, but with a thick, toxic atmosphere.

Earth

The only planet known to support life, with abundant water and a breathable atmosphere.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet, it's home to the largest volcano and canyon in the solar system.

Jupiter

The largest planet in our solar system, known for its Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Famous for its stunning rings made of ice and rock particles.

Uranus

A gas giant with a unique sideways rotation and faint rings.

Neptune

The farthest planet from the Sun, known for its strong winds and deep blue color.
